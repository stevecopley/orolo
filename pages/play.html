I Ride Bikes

I've loved riding bikes all of my life. I discovered mountain biking whilst at university in 1986, and I've been riding trails ever since. It's fair to say that MTBs have been a major part of my adult life, leading to many great friendships, and some amazing adventures.

I presently ride a Yeti SB150, fully kitted out with Hope components, carbon wheels, etc. It's beautiful and I love it. It's the perfect bike for the trails around Nelson.
