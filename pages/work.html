Digital Technologies

At work you will find me teaching students from Years 9 to 13. I love teaching Digital Technologies; it's great fun trying to pass on my enthusiasm for nerdy things.

My classes cover topics such as:

  • - The impact of technology
  • - Computer Science theory
  • - Design theory
  • - Algorithmic thinking
  • - Programming (Scratch, Python, JS, Kotlin, etc.)
  • - Front-end web development (HTML, CSS, JS)
  • - Back-end web development (PHP, MySQL)
  • - The design process
  • - Simple animation

Effective Pedagogy

I'm keen to understand how I can be a more impactful educator. I am interested in effective pedagogy and I push myself to explore different learning strategies and formats for my classes.

Many of the teaching tools and resources I have developed for my classroom over the years are based on teaching and learning strategies I have read about, and wish to put into practice.
